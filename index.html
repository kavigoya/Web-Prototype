OUR STORY

Welcome to Giardina Gelati's website!

With over 40 years experience, Giardina Gelati has created a name for itself through its authentic Italian ice cream using fresh ingredients. We offer a wide range of ice cream and sorbet flavours catering to all dietary restrictions and preferences to make more health conscious decisions. Not only do we sell tasty ice cream, but also delicious cakes and pastries. We prepare all of our goodies with fresh and healthy ingredients, the Italian style. Get ready to be blown away by our sweet treats.

Now operating at 4 locations on the Gold Coast and available for delivery! See what our brand has to offer, we look forward to seeing you soon!
